Eco Tips Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Eco Tips is an offline-first application designed to provide curated sustainability tips without collecting any personal information.

1. No Personal Data Collection
Eco Tips does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from users.

2. Offline-First
All content is bundled within the app. No network connection is required to use the app's core features.

3. Optional Crash Reporting
Users may opt-in to enable crash reporting. This feature only collects technical crash data and does not include any user content or personal data.

4. Permissions
The app requests no permissions beyond those essential for operation. No access to camera, location, or storage is required.

5. Data Security
All data is stored locally on the user's device. No data is shared externally.

6. Updates
Future content updates will be signed and verified to ensure integrity and authenticity.

For questions or concerns, please contact support@ecotips.example
